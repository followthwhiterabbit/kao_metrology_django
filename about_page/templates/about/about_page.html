ABOUT US

KAO was founded in Istanbul in 1995 by an idealistic and dynamic team with twenty years of experience in machinery and electronics. The company aimed to produce technological devices needed by the industry. With a strong R&D team and modern production lines, KAO has been able to create remarkable products in terms of technical specifications, ergonomics, and aesthetics to keep up with the incredible pace of today's technology. The company has successfully produced test devices with measurement precision beyond world standards and extremely user-friendly interfaces in the field of metrology. As KAO-Metroloji, we aim to collaborate with industrialists to contribute to creating a more efficient and powerful future. Therefore, we see every product we create as a helper that opens the door to new possibilities. Please don't hesitate to share your thoughts on our products and technologies encountered on our website. Wishing for every tomorrow to be brighter than today...
