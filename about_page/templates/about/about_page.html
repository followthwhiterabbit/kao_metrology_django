HISTORY

About Us

Kao was founded in Istanbul in 1995 by an idelistic and dynamic team with thirty years of experience in machinery, electronics, and metrology. With a strong R&D team and modern production lines, Kao has been able to create remarkable products in terms of the technical specifications, ergonomics, aesthetics, and durability. Kao has been producing metrological test devices with measurement precision beyond world standards and extremely user-friendly interfaces in the field of metrology As Kao-Metrology, we aim to collaborate with industrialists to contribute to creating a more efficient and powerful metrological system for a reliable future. Therefore, we see every product we develop as a helper that opens the door to new possibilities.
